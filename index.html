Privacy Policy
Dice Thrower

Last updated: April 24, 2026

Dice Thrower does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. No information ever leaves your device.

Overview

Dice Thrower is a browser extension that simulates rolling dice. This policy describes what data the extension does and does not collect.

Data Collection

Dice Thrower does not collect, transmit, store, or share any personal data or usage information. Specifically:

  • No analytics or tracking of any kind
  • No crash reporting sent to external servers
  • No identifiers — device, user, or session — collected
  • No network requests made by the extension at any time

Local Storage Only

The extension saves your current dice configuration (which dice are selected, quantity of each, and modifier value) locally in your browser using chrome.storage.local. This data:

  • Never leaves your device
  • Is not accessible to any server or third party
  • Can be cleared at any time by uninstalling the extension or clearing browser extension storage

Permissions

The extension requests the following browser permissions:

  • storage — to save your dice configuration between sessions (local only)

No permission is used to access browsing history, page content, or any other browser data.

Third Parties

Dice Thrower does not integrate with any third-party services, APIs, or advertising networks.

Changes

If this policy is updated, the "Last updated" date above will change. Given that the extension collects no data, substantive changes to this policy are unlikely.
